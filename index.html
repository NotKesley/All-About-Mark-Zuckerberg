All About Mark Zuckerberg

Who Is Mark Zuckerberg

Age 39

Mark Zuckerberg worked out of his Harvard University dorm room to co-found the social networking site Facebook. After his sophomore year, Zuckerberg dropped out of college to focus on the website, which now has over two billion users, making him a multibillionaire. The 2010 movie The Social Network depicted the creation of Facebook.

Early Life

Age 1-13

His Education

Age 14

The Facebook was founded by Mark Zuckerberg, Dustin Moskovitz, Chris Hughes, and Eduardo Saverin. It was a website where users could make their own accounts, upload pictures, and interact with other users. Up until June 2004, the crew maintained the website from a Harvard University dorm room. In that same year, Zuckerberg left college and relocated the business to Palo Alto, California. One million people were using Facebook by the end of 2004.
